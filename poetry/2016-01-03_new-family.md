# New Family
_16 July 2017_
```
Maybe I can begin to understand
What it feels like to be adopted.
There are many kind faces here,
Changing, smiling,
But I don't know them.
I have begun my knowledge of this house.
But I don't know it.
New family, new house,
This is my name.
```
