# Tears felt appropriate
_27 January 2006_
```
Tears felt appropriate to the plight of two persons.
They were huddled
hands clasped and heads bowed.
She was confused by glass and smoke.
He sat
and the eyes would blink for her
had the wheel rotated in air.
```
