# His blood fell to soil
_May 2006_
```
His blood fell to soil
First through the pore
He cherished us with toil
In a garden, and on a cross of trees
He saved His people
Calling open armed,
Pleading, “Come unto me.”
```
—[Tod Robbins](http://todrobbins.com)
